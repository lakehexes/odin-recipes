Foraged mushrooms: Chicken of the Woods

Description

A bright yellow mushroom which grows in shelves on trees. Easy to identify and very tasty!

  • A kg of freshly foraged chicken of the woods
  • One large onion
  • Three cloves garlic, crushed
  • Juice of one lemon
  • 250ml white wine
  1. Clean mushrooms gently yet thoroughly using a mushroom brush
  2. Slice onion and fry in vegetable oil on medium heat
  3. Add crushed garlic and sliced mushrooms
  4. Allow to fry until mushrooms and onions appear golden brown
  5. Add wine to simmer
  6. Mix in lemon juice to taste
