Foraged mushrooms: Hedgehog Fungus

Description

This is a light coloured mushrooms with spines instead of gills. It has a nutty taste and crunchy texture.

  • 300g hedgehog fungus
  • Tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Three cloves garlic, crushed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Clean mushrooms gently yet thoroughly using a mushroom brush
  2. Fry chopped mushrooms in vegtable oil
  3. Add garlic and fry until mushrooms are golden
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
