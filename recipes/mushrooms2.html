Foraged mushrooms: Chanterelles

Foraged mushrooms:Chanterelles

Description

A golden funnel shaped mushrooms which smells of apricots. Take care not to mix this mushroom up with its poisonous lookalike, the false chanterelle.

  • 500g freshly foraged chanterelle mushrooms
  • Tablespoon olive oil
  • One large onion
  • One can butter beans
  • Tablespoon tomato puree
  1. Wash and chop chanterelle mushrooms
  2. Dice onion and fry in oil until brown
  3. Add chanterelle mushrooms and tomato puree
  4. Fry on medium heat until mushrooms are brown
  5. Stir in butter beans and heat for a further ten minutes
